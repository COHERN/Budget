Help

Quick Check: Type your current balance and any planned purchase. We read unpaid bills from the Bills page and show how much is left, so impulse buys get a reality check.

Bills: Add each bill. Use the DATE picker, enter the AMOUNT, and tick PAID when done. Everything saves locally on your device.

50/30/20: Enter income to see the classic split.

Privacy: No servers. Data lives in your browser localStorage (bt.bills.v2).
